Our History

The restaurant Campbells was founded in the year 1752, after John Campbell invested in turning an old building into one of the most popular restaurants found in South Philadelphia. Throughout the years, the restaurant has grown and expanded significantly. In the past years, it has became a culinary landmark known for its signature dishes, elegant atmosphere, and top-notch service. Campbells has consistently adapted to changing times and tastes, evolving to meet the needs of generations of diners while preserving its rich legacy. Today, Campbells stands not only as a restaurant but as a historic icon in the city.

Looking for Work

Campbells restaurant has been at the heart of culinary excellence since its founding in 1752. We believe our team members are the heart our restaurant, and we're always looking for passionate individuals to join the team. If you are enthusiastic about hospitality, dedicated to quality service, and eager to be a part of a dynamic and historic team, we'd love to hear from you about how you can join the team!
Reach out personally to the owner at campbellj19@lasalle.edu to explore opportunities and be a part of the team that continues to shape South Philadelphia's dining experience.

Community Involvement

At Campbells, it is not just about us, we also look to improve the community. Over the years, we've partnered with local businesses, contributed to food drives, and have supported numerous community events in All of Philadelphia. From helping local neighborhoods, working with other dinners, Campbells actively engages in initiatives that make a difference. We're committed to giving back to the community that has supported us for centuries. Whether it's through sustainable sourcing or educational programs, we're here to nurture and uplift the neighborhood we call home. Whether they want it or not.
